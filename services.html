FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What kinds of events do you perform at?

Whimsy Entertainment is available for children’s birthday parties, family events, school and library shows, community festivals, corporate events, holiday parties, nightlife and concerts, private parties and more. If your event could use live circus, clown, mime, fire or variety entertainment in Northeast Ohio (near Cleveland & Columbus) across Northeast Ohio (near Cleveland & Columbus), plus Detroit and Pittsburgh, we can probably help.

What acts do you offer?

Saria “Blu” Braska offers a wide range of circus and party entertainment: giant bubble shows, face painting, balloon twisting, juggling, clowning, miming, LED manipulation and fire object manipulation with props like levi wand and buugeng, fire eating, fire breathing and circus sideshow acts like the very dangerous Blockhead and world renown bed of nails. We can build a lineup that fits your audience, venue and comfort level.

Do you travel outside of Northeast Ohio (near Cleveland & Columbus)?

Yes. Whimsy Entertainment is based in Northeast Ohio (near Cleveland & Columbus) but can travel across Ohio, including Detroit and Pittsburgh area bookings, and to other states for the right event. Travel fees may apply depending on distance and schedule. Let us know your city and venue and we’ll include travel details in your quote.

Are fire and sideshow acts safe?

Safety is always the priority. Fire eating, fire breathing and fire object manipulation are only performed where venue rules, weather, space and safety conditions allow. Circus sideshow acts such as blockhead and bed of nails are performed by an experienced professional with over 10 years on stage. We are happy to discuss what is appropriate for your audience.

What do you need from the venue or host?

Requirements depend on the acts you choose. For most kids party and roaming sets (bubbles, balloons, face painting, juggling and clowning) we mainly need enough space to safely move around, a small setup area and access to shade or indoor space in extreme weather. Fire and LED shows may require a clear performance area, distance from the audience and permission from the venue.

How far in advance should we book?

Weekends and holidays can book up quickly, especially for children’s parties and festivals. Booking a few weeks to a few months in advance is ideal, but last-minute openings do happen. Contact us with your date, time and location and we’ll let you know current availability.
